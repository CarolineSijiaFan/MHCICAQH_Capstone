About CAQH

The Council for Affordable Quality Healthcare (CAQH) was formed by health plans who came together nearly 25 years ago with the desire to make healthcare work better. Today, CAQH is trusted by stakeholders from across the healthcare ecosystem to improve business processes and offer the most comprehensive provider and member data in the US.

4.8M+

provider records in the CAQH database for credentialing, directory management, and more

1,000+

healthcare organizations use CAQH member and provider data solutions to improve business processes

235M+

member records served by payers that use CAQH solutions to improve payment integrity

CAQH's Mission

CAQH's mission is to make healthcare work better by simplifying and automating administrative processes. As a non-profit organization, CAQH serves as a trusted intermediary that helps reduce administrative burden while improving data quality and accessibility across the healthcare ecosystem.

Key Services:

  • Provider Data Management: Comprehensive database of healthcare provider information
  • Credentialing Solutions: Streamlined processes for provider verification and credentialing
  • Directory Management: Accurate, up-to-date provider directories for health plans
  • Payment Integrity: Solutions to improve accuracy and efficiency in healthcare payments

About the MHCI Program

The Master of Human-Computer Interaction (MHCI) program at Carnegie Mellon University is the first program in the world dedicated to preparing professionals for careers related to human-computer interaction, user experience design and user-centered research.

The MHCI program integrates service and design thinking into a rigorous HCI curriculum that prepares our students to design and guide the future of human and technology interactions.

Industry Leadership

First HCI master's program globally, setting the standard for human-computer interaction education for over three decades.

Rigorous Curriculum

Combines theoretical foundations with hands-on project experience, including industry partnerships and real-world problem solving.

Design Thinking Focus

Emphasizes user-centered design, service design, and systems thinking to create meaningful technological solutions.

Capstone Experience

Year-long partnerships with industry leaders provide students with opportunities to tackle complex, real-world challenges.
